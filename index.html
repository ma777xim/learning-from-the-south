What we cover

Modules
01 — Analysis

Sensor & weather data

A slot for time-series panels: EPW weather files, on-site loggers, or simulation output. Drop a chart library in and point it at this card grid.

02 — Simulation

Thermal modeling

Surface temperature fields, energy balance terms, stability diagnostics — anything coming out of a Grasshopper/Python pipeline fits here.

03 — Comparison

Strategy benchmarking

Side-by-side cards for comparing mitigation strategies, material choices, or vernacular vs. mechanical cooling approaches.

Note: This is a prototype for our thesis project, focused on developing a web-based dashboard for climate data visualization and analysis. Content is to be updated with data as the project progresses.
